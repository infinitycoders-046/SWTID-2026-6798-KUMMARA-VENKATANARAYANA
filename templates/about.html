Project Overview

Comprehensive Analysis and Dietary Strategies with Tableau: A College Food Choices Case Study

This project analyzes college students' food habits, dietary preferences, nutritional awareness, and lifestyle patterns using Tableau dashboards. The interactive visualizations provide meaningful insights that help promote healthier eating habits and support data-driven decision making.
